Squat Exercise

Start Calibration

Instructions:

1. Calibration Phase Introduction: The exercise will begin with a calibration phase.

2. Isolation Requirement: Please ensure the camera only captures you. There shouldn't be anyone else in the camera's field of view.

3. Posture and Position Requirement: Stand facing the camera with your entire body, including your feet, clearly visible.

4. Squat Pose Calibration: In the initial stage of calibration, you'll need to assume a squat pose once a yellow box appears on the screen. Maintain this pose for approximately 5 seconds. As soon as the box turns green, return to an upright stance
